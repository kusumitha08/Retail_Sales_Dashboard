📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview:

This project presents a Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI.
It provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and future trends.

🎯 Objective:

* Analyze retail sales data.
* Identify business trends.
* Understand customer behavior.
* Perform 15-day sales forecasting.

📊 Key Metrics:

* 💰 Total Sales: 1.57M.
* 📦 Quantity Sold: 22K.
* 📈 Total Profit: 175K.

📈 Dashboard Features:

* 📅 Monthly Sales Trend (YOY).
* 🌍 Sales & Profit by State.
* 🛒 Sales by Category & Sub-Category.
* 👥 Sales by Segment.
* 🚚 Sales by Ship Mode.
* 💳 Sales by Payment Mode.
* 🔮 Sales Forecast (15 Days).
